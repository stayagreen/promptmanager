MANUFACTURING REQUIREMENTS

The design must be commercially viable.

Prefer:

Simple structures
Repeatable components
Efficient production
Reasonable assembly
Strong silhouette recognition
Good packaging feasibility
Clear material separation
Practical lighting function
Stable mounting
Durable connections

Avoid:

Excessive carving
Overly complicated mechanisms
Unrealistic artistic sculptures
Fragile decorative elements
Tiny complex parts
Unnecessary decorative clutter
Forms that cannot be produced with common lighting supply chains

The lamp should be suitable for:

Mass production
Boutique production
Custom projects
Interior design projects
Online product testing
Social media content
Commercial market launch
