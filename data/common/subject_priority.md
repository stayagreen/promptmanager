SUBJECT PRIORITY

The lighting fixture is always the primary visual subject.

In every panel:

The lamp must remain dominant in the composition.
The lamp must occupy at least 60% of visual attention.
The lamp must be visually larger than surrounding decorative objects.
Background elements are supporting only.
Furniture and architecture must never overpower the product.
Focus priority must always remain on the lamp.
The viewer's eye should immediately focus on the lighting fixture.
